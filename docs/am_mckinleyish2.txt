Notes:
Created from scratch, another homage to the McKinley series for Quake/Quake II CTF. 

Content:
Weapons: 2x Shotgun, 2x Rocket Launcher, 2x Plasma Gun, 2x Railgun, 2x Nailgun, Chaingun.
Items: 2x Combat Armor, 1x Body Armor.
Holdables & Powerups: 1x Quad Damage, 1x Invulnerability
Runes: 2x Doubler, 2x Guard, 2x Ammo Regen, 2x Scout

Gametypes: CTF, 1FCTF, Harvester, Overload, Elimination, CTF Elimination, DD
Version: a2
