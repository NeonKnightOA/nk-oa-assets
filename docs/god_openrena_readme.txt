GOD_OPENARENA (GoDs Open Anrena)

Map released under GPLv2 or later.

A fun map made for the GoD Clan supporting the following game types:
* ffa ctf oneflag elimination ctfelim dom dd obelisk/harvester overload

Content:
* Weapons: 2 Grenade Launcher, 2 Lightning Guns, 2 Nailguns, 2 Plasmaguns, 2 Railguns, 6 Rocket launcher, 4 Shotguns
* Items: 6 Yellow Cross Bubble, 2 Yellow Armor
