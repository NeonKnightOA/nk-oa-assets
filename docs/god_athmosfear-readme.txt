GOD_ATHMOSFEAR (GoDs Athmosfear)

Map released under GPLv2 or later.

A heavily modified redo from scratch of oa_bases7 made for the GoD Clan.

* Weapons: 

Building:
* q3map2 -meta
* q3map2 -vis -saveptr
* q3map2 -light -fast -super 2 -filter -bounce 8
* bspc -forcesidesvisible -optimize -bsp2aas
