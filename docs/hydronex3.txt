Notes:
Fixes & changes done to hydronex, inspired by CTF-Hydro16 for Unreal tournament, originally made by Maik "SavageX" Merten for Nexuiz, and adapted to OA by leileilol. It's a hugely different map.

Gametypes: CTF, 1FCTF, Harvester, Overload, Elimination, CTF Elimination, DD, Domination (5 points)
Version: a1

Content:
* Weapons: 2x Shotgun, 4x Grenade Launcher, 2x Rocket Launcher, 2x Plasma Gun, Railgun, 2x Lightning Gun, 2x Nailgun, 2x Chaingun, Prox Launcher.
* Items: 2x Combat Armor, 2x Body Armor, 
* Runes: 4x Scout, 4X Guard, 4x Doubler
* Powerups: Regeneration, Quad Damage, 2x Battle Suit.
