GOD_OASAGO2 (Sago´s Castle - GoD Edition)

Map released under GPLv2.

A special edition of oasago2 (based upon oasago2f9_hintbrushes2) made for the GoD Clan.

Content:
* Weapons: 4x Shotgun, 2x Grenade Launcher, 5x Rocket Launcher, 4x Plasma Gun, 2x Railgun, 2x Lightning Gun.
* Items: 1x Combat Armor. And plenty of health & armor.
