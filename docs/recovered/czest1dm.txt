Notes:
Created originally by Adam "Czestmyr" Vojtech, with improvements from Poul "sago007" Sanders. It was improved brushwork-wise, and has support for Elimination and Single Domination.

Content:
* Weapons: Shotgun x3, Grenade Launcher x2, Rocket Launcher x3, Lightning Gun x1, Railgun x1, Plasma Gun x2.
* Powerups: Quad Damage

Gametypes: FFA, Tourney, Elimination, LMS, Domination (3 points)
Version: a1 (started from the SVN version in 0.8.5)
